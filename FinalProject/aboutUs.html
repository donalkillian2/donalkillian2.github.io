Georgia Bird Rehab is an organisation dedecated to the rescse, care, rehabilition and preservation of all Georgia wild birds. Georgia Bird Rehab facility at 250 ketton downs, Duluth, GA amits injured, orphaned and abandoned songbirds and corvids to rehabilitate back to their native habitats. Raptors and Waterfoul are also helped by sending them to specilized Raptors and Waterfoul rehab facility.

Another goal is to educate the genral public about the importance of all GA birds and their native habitats. We promote, not cutting down trees in the spring so that parent birds can rais their chicks. Not using petisides so parent birds can feed their chicks. Keeping all cats indoors so fledglings can learn to fly. Human activity and free roaming cats are responsable for the declining bird population in Georgia.

Georgia Bird Rehab is a fully donation funded and voluntear run facility liciend by GA DMR - (Department of Natral Resoures)
